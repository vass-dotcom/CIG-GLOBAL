Our Zanzibar Investment
A World-Class Destination

CIG holds a strategic partnership with the Ministry of Tourism (Zanzibar) to develop premium tourism infrastructure, marine conservation zones, and integrated smart city clusters along Zanzibar's pristine coastline.

Our investment thesis positions Zanzibar as the premier luxury eco-tourism destination in East Africa while simultaneously building the digital infrastructure of a 21st-century smart city.
