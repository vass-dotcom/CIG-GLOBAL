🇦🇪 Dubai, UAE

Dubai serves as CIG's strategic nerve center for the Middle East and Gulf region — a gateway that connects our global energy portfolio with the world's most dynamic capital markets and sovereign wealth ecosystems.

From Dubai, CIG coordinates energy finance, facilitates cross-border investment structuring, and manages clean technology partnerships with regional governments and international development institutions.

The UAE's forward-thinking regulatory environment, world-class infrastructure, and proximity to key energy markets across Africa, Asia, and the Gulf make it the ideal hub for CIG's regional ambitions.

What CIG Does
in Dubai

Our Dubai office bridges global capital with high-impact energy and infrastructure projects across emerging markets.

🏦

Energy Finance & Structuring

CIG structures complex cross-border energy financing deals, connecting sovereign wealth funds and institutional investors with high-yield infrastructure projects across Africa and Asia.

🌿

Clean Technology Investment

We identify, evaluate, and deploy capital into cutting-edge clean energy technologies — from advanced solar storage to hydrogen infrastructure — aligned with UAE Net Zero 2050 goals.

🛢️

Oil & Gas Asset Management

Strategic oversight of CIG's oil and gas assets across Indonesia, Venezuela, and Gabon, coordinated from Dubai's hub with access to DIFC financial infrastructure.

🤝

Government Partnerships

Direct engagement with UAE federal authorities and Abu Dhabi Development Holding to align CIG projects with national development vision and bilateral agreements.

📊

Regional Market Intelligence

Real-time monitoring of Gulf energy markets, commodity flows, and geopolitical developments to inform CIG's global investment and operational decisions.

🚀

Innovation & Venture

CIG's Dubai team scouts emerging energy tech ventures and startup ecosystems through GITEX, Abu Dhabi Sustainability Week, and direct VC partnerships in the UAE innovation corridor.

CIG Dubai
Key Initiatives

01

DIFC Investment Corridors

Leveraging Dubai International Financial Centre's regulatory framework to structure and close cross-border energy investment transactions with speed and compliance.

02

Solar & Renewable Procurement

Coordinating large-scale solar equipment procurement and technology licensing deals between Asian manufacturers and CIG's African and Latin American project sites.

03

Africa–Gulf Energy Bridge

Creating a financial and logistical bridge between Gulf capital and Africa's energy infrastructure deficit — channeling investment into Gabon, Guinea, and Central Africa operations.

04

Carbon Credit Framework

Developing carbon trading mechanisms aligned with COP28 legacy goals, positioning CIG's renewable projects to generate and monetize verified carbon credits in Gulf markets.

05

Petrochemical Trade Routes

Managing strategic petrochemical trading flows between CIG's oil and gas assets in Southeast Asia and Venezuela, routed through Dubai's commodity trading infrastructure.

06

Smart Grid Technology Transfer

Facilitating technology transfer agreements between UAE-based smart grid companies and CIG's smart city and energy projects in Indonesia and East Africa.
