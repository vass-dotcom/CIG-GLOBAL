Overview
Zanzibar — A World-Class Destination

CIG (Zanzibar, Tanzania) is a company focused on developing luxury coastal resorts, marine conservation zones, and integrated tourism infrastructure in one of East Africa's most iconic destinations. Its mission is to create world-class experiences that blend high-end hospitality with environmental stewardship. By combining sustainable design with strategic development, CIG enhances the region's global appeal while preserving its natural beauty. The company positions itself as a key player in responsible tourism development.

Through its projects in Zanzibar, CIG develops premium resorts, protected marine areas, and supporting infrastructure designed to elevate the visitor experience. These initiatives integrate eco-friendly construction, biodiversity preservation, and community engagement to ensure long-term sustainability. By aligning tourism growth with conservation efforts, CIG helps protect fragile coastal and marine ecosystems. Each development is crafted to balance luxury, authenticity, and environmental responsibility.

CIG also plays a strategic role in strengthening the local economy by creating jobs and supporting regional development. By investing in integrated tourism ecosystems, the company promotes cultural heritage, local entrepreneurship, and international visibility. Its vision is to establish Zanzibar as a leading global destination for sustainable luxury tourism.
