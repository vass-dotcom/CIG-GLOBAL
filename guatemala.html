Overview
Zanzibar — A Strategic Investment

CIG holds a strategic partnership with the Ministry of Tourism (Zanzibar) to develop premium tourism infrastructure, marine conservation zones, and integrated smart city clusters along Zanzibar's pristine coastline. Our investment positions Zanzibar as the premier luxury eco-tourism destination in East Africa.

The archipelago's unique combination of white sand beaches, Swahili cultural heritage, and proximity to major global routes makes it one of the most compelling tourism investment opportunities in the African continent.
